About Me

M.S. AI @ NCKU MMCV Lab | Research Student

Hello there!

I'm Liu Shih-Wen 劉世文 (Casper). I'm a research student in the Multimedia & Computer Vision Lab at National Cheng Kung University, mentored by Prof. Wei-Ta Chu. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from National Sun Yat-sen University in 2024. I worked on two graduate research projects under Prof. Shu-Min Li and Prof. Wen-Hsiang Lu.

My core interests are efficient and adaptive AI. I particularly focus on designing parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT), adaptive mechanisms for multi-task learning (MTL) vision model, large multimodal model (LMM) and histopathology report generation.
